         <h3>Mobile Moves Michigan<br/>  Business and Professional Event</h3>This event spans the period from  2012-07-30 to 2012-07-31 <br />Mobile Technology is Exploding!<br /><br />



 



It's changing the way we live and the way we run our businesses. But mobile technology is much more than the latest 'app' downloaded to your mobile device; mobile is actually many technologies that are impacting every vertical doing business in Michigan. The mobile / wireless industry is one of only four global industries that generate over one trillion dollars in revenue annually, and in Michigan, it's one of our fastest-growing sectors - but yet the best kept secret in the state. Mobile technologies have the ability to transform our state's economy - if we act now!<br /><br />



 



Learn more at<br />



Mobile Moves Michigan<br />



 



 



This ground-breaking statewide event will have something for everyone interested in mobile / wireless technologies!<br />



 



 



Who Should Attend?<br /><br />



    C-level executives from Michigan businesses; all verticals, including but not limited to:  general manufacturing, automotive, healthcare, financial services, legal, non-profit, staffing, business services, consumer services, retail, utilities AND MUCH MORE!<br />

    Those interested in learning about the opportunities mobile technologies provide to increase productivity and profitability for their businesses and  industry verticals<br />

    Those working in any sector of the mobile / wireless industry

    Those interested in learning about the types of mobile / wireless companies currently creating jobs and positive economic impact in Michigan<br />

    Individuals interested in learning how mobile technologies can substantially grow Michigan's economy<br />

    Senior economic development officials<br />

    Local and state government officials (elected and appointed)<br />

    Administrators from Michigan school districts and higher-education institutions<br />

    Senior executives from Michigan-based foundations<br />

    Senior executives from Michigan-based associations/organizations; particularly those focused on Michigan revitalization efforts<br />

    Educators providing mobile development coursework to students<br />

    Educators interested in utilizing mobile technologies within their classrooms<br />

    Mobile software developers<br />

    Students interested in exploring mobile development as a career opportunity<br />

    Corporate and small business marketers, advertising and public relations agencies<br /><br />
