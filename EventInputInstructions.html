Enter Event Information
Go to the "Whats happening This week"page and find the button name"Future Events". Then click on the button

Fill out the form and submit it. An email message will be sent to the webmaster with the information

The address of the page is http://www.graynwhite.com/whtw/whevents.php you can click on the hyperlink on the right

Get Future Event Information
Go to the "Whats Happening This Week" and use the button entitled Add/Change
A clalendar of the current month will be presented
If you want to see the detail for a particular day click on the day number in the upper left corner of the day and the detail will be displayed for that day. Use the back button of your browser to get back to the monthly calendar.
To get to another month, look at the bottom left corner of the monthly calendar and select another mont and/or year
